Dr. Savitha DR

M.D (Dermatology)

Consultant Cosmetic Dermatologist

Skin is the largest organ of the body. It covers and protects all other organs. Skin is the most important organ and one reason... for beauty. Without skin we all probably, look alike. At Dr. Savitha's Skin Care Clinic, we offer you the tips and techniques to keep your skin healthy and beautiful.In addition to treating various skin diseases, we offer you different Cosmetic procedures.

Dr.Savitha has studied in Father Muller Medical College at Mangalore.

She has worked on Psoriasis and Phototherapy. She has undergone training in Cosmetic Dermatology procedures including use of Laser in various skin diseases. She performs Radio Surgery, Electro Cautery, Chemical peels, Punch graft, Dermaroller and various laser procedures.
