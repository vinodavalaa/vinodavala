PSYCHOLOGY

Psychology is the study of how the mind instantiated in the brain enables organism to act, think and feel. In case of humans it examines the nature of cognitive, emotional and motivational process that allow them to anticipate and react to the opportunities afforded by the world in which they live – a highly complex world of social structures, other minds, cultural influences and normative pressures.

At Chethana Neuro Center we help a wide variety of people facing many kinds of problems like depression, being angry or anxious for a long time. Sometimes people approach us when they want help for a chronic condition that is interfering with their lives or physical health or others may have short term problems for which they may want help navigating such as feeling overwhelmed by a new job or grieving death of family member.

We also help people:

  • Learn to cope with stressful situations
  • Overcome addictions
  • Manage their chronic illness
  • Break past the barriers that keep them from reaching their goals etc

We interpret a number of tests and assessments that can help diagnose a condition or tell more about the way a person thinks, feels and behaves. These tests may evaluate:

  • Intellectual Skills
  • Cognitive Strengths And Weakness
  • Vocational Aptitude And Preference
  • Personality Characteristics
  • Neuropsychological Functions etc

Few cases may need Psychotherapy along with Counseling and Medications. Psychotherapy or talk therapy is a way to help people with a broad variety of mental illness and emotional difficulties. Few problems that can be addressed by Psychotherapy include:

  • Difficulties in coping with daily life
  • Impact of trauma
  • Medical illness or loss (like death of loved ones)
  • Specific mental disorders like Depression, Anxiety etc

Here at Chethana Neuro Center we provide different Psychotherapies like Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Interpersonal Therapy, Dialectical Behavior Therapy, Psychodynamic Therapy, Psychoanalysis, Supportive Therapy etc. Depending on the needs of the person necessary therapy is provided based on his/her illness, circumstances and preference.
