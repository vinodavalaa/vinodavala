PREETHI S S

PGDCHC (Child Counseling), B.Tech, M.S

Mrs. Preethi S S wife of Dr. Manu A N worked as a Software Engineer for 4.5 years at Bangalore of which 2.5 years was in orientation and training sector. She pursued her MS while working and parallely conducted part time courses at Bangalore.

She worked as a part time lecturer in Govt Science College for the next 2 years during which she also worked as a part time teacher in a private school at Hassan.

Her keen interest in Psychology and her passion for teaching led her to pursue Post Graduate Diploma in Child Health and Counseling at Manasa Educational Foundation for Mental Health, Shimoga.After having successfully completed Internship at Manasa she is currently practising as a Child Counselor at Chethana Neuro Center and following her passion by teaching in school.
