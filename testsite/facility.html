PHARMACY

CHAYA MEDICALS at CNC was established in year 2005 and is one of Hassan’s leading Medical Store in dispensing drugs related to Psychiatric and Neurological disorders. Our Pharmacy offers a wide selection of genuine medicines stored under appropriate storage conditions at best market prices. We do provide medicines at a discounted price for the needy.

DAY CARE UNIT

Day Care Facility at Chethana Neuro Center is provided for clinical treatment, assessment and maintenance of function of patients in particular though not exclusively but for cases which need observation like Anxiety, Maniac’s, Depression etc.

INTERNSHIP

Chethana Neuro Center also offers Internship Programs for Master of Social Work students and for others on referrals from Institutions.
