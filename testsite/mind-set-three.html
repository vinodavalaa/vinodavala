Each person has three important functions of mind namely:
  • Cognition
  • Action
  • Emotion

Cognition is the psychological process involved in acquisition and understanding of knowledge through thought, experience and senses. These are core skills your brain uses to think, read, learn, concentrate, remember, reason, judge and pay attention. It encompasses many aspects of intellectual functions and processes such as attention, the formation of knowledge, judgment and evaluation, memory and working memory, reasoning and "computation", problem solving and decision making, comprehension and production of language. Cognitive processes use existing knowledge and generate new knowledge.

Action is the fact or process of doing something, typically to active an aim, a thing done, an act, the way in which something works or move.

Emotion is often the driving force behind motivation, positive or negative. It is the mental state associated with the nervous system brought on by chemical changes variously associated with thoughts, feelings, behavioral responses, and a degree of pleasure or displeasure.

When there is normality in the above functions of mind, a person is said to lead a healthy and balanced life. A slight impairment in them may lead to mental illness. Although the exact cause of most mental illnesses is not known, it is becoming clear through research that many of these conditions are caused by a combination of biological, psychological, and environmental factors.

We at Chethana Neuro Center having a deep understanding of physical and mental health – and how they affect each other assess all of your mental and physical symptoms. We make a diagnosis and work with you to develop a management plan for your treatment and recovery. We provide treatment, prescribe medications, do procedures such as electroconvulsive therapy along with below services:

  • Provide urgent care for a sudden mental illness.
  • Help you to manage a long-term mental health condition.
  • Provide advice about lifestyle changes.
  • Work with you individually, or with you and your partner, family or careers.
  • Provide second opinions and advice to other doctors and health professionals.
  • Refer you to other health professionals
  • Admit you to hospital if required etc

OUR MISSION

We believe that Chethana Neuro Center, Hassan’s first clinic dedicated to treat Neurological and Psychiatric disorders, has a responsibility to:

  • Ensure access to superior quality integrated health care for our community and expand health care access for populations in need within the community.
  • Create a supportive team environment for patients, employees, and clinical staff.

OUR VISION

Chethana Neuro Center will continue to provide a continuum of high-quality, cost-effective services in order to improve the health status of those we serve. In providing these services, we strive to operate at the highest level of personal and business integrity.

OUR VALUES

Chethana Neuro Center holds the below values to be fundamental:

  • Service - We provide every patient and his or her loved ones with unparalleled service in an environment that is both responsive and healing
  • Enthusiasm - We remember that the true meaning of this value is "to be inspired" and we do our jobs with the same spirit and commitment that first inspired us to work in health care.
  • Respect - We promise to honor the dignity, individuality and rights of everyone, and to pay particular attention to patients' rights to privacy, confidentiality and information.
  • Value - We offer care, services and technology of the highest quality at the lowest possible cost. We are responsible to the community for the appropriate use of its gifts, resources and support.
