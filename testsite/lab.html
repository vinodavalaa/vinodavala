EEG

Electroencephalography is an electrophysiological monitoring method to record electrical activity of the brain. Dr.A.M.Nagesh was the first person from Mental Health Department at Hassan to install EEG machine and perform EEG test in the year 1985.

EEG detects electrical activity using small metal discs attached to scalp. Brain cells communicate via electrical impulses and are active all time even when a person is asleep. This brain activity shows up as wavy lines on EEG recording.

EEG is safe and painless and plays a key role in recording brain disorders like:

  • Brain Tumor
  • Brain Damage from head injury
  • Brain Dysfunction that can have variety of causes (Encephalopathy)
  • Inflammation of brain (Encephalitis)
  • Stroke
  • Sleep Disorders
  • Epilepsy or Seizure Disorders
  • Mood Disorders

ECG

Electrocardiogram (ECG) in Chethana Neuro center was started in the year 2019. ECG is a medical test that detects heart problems by measuring the electrical activity generated by the heart as it contracts. ECG’s from healthy hearts have a characteristic shape. If the ECG shows a different shape it could suggest a heart problem. ECG is safe and non invasive procedure.

ECG is recommended for people who may be at risk of heart disease because they have:

  • Family history of heart disease
  • Smoking habits
  • Overweight issues
  • Diabetic
  • High cholesterol
  • High Blood pressure

Other symptoms for which ECG is recommended are for people with conditions such as chest pain, breathlessness, dizziness, fainting, fast or irregular heartbeats etc.

BIO FEEDBACK

Biofeedback helps patient to make subtle changes in their body, such as relaxing certain muscles, to achieve the results they want, such as reducing pain etc. It gives the ability to practice new ways to control the body, often to improve a health condition or physical performance.

Biofeedback methods used at Chethana Neuro Center are Breathing, Heart rate and Muscle contraction based on patient’s health problems. Common indications to go for biofeedback are:

  • Anxiety or stress
  • Asthma
  • Attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder (ADHD)
  • Chronic pain
  • Constipation
  • Fecal incontinence
  • Fibromyalgia
  • Headache
  • High blood pressure
  • Ringing in the ears (tinnitus)
  • Stroke

Other symptoms for which ECG is recommended are for people with conditions such as chest pain, breathlessness, dizziness, fainting, fast or irregular heartbeats etc.
